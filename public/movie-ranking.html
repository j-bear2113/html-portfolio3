The Best Horror Movies According to Joel

My top 3 horror movies of the year

Barbarian

The less you know going in, the better. Going in to the movie I was not expecting it to play out the way it did. A fun and creepy adventure!

The Taking of Deborah Logan

I watched this movie home alone, and I was legit creeped out and scared afterward.

Oddity

A suspenseful slow burn that is guranteed to make you feel uncomfortable at least a few time while viewing.
